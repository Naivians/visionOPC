History & Philosophy

VISION INTERNATIONAL CONSTRUCTION OPC is a privately owned company that was established last September 2020.

It takes on the role of Importer, Supplier, Trading Business, and Construction Services for small to medium projects and performs project management services to coordinates specialist trade for industrial and commercial projects.

We also provide design outputs and engineering solutions as value-added services to clients.
